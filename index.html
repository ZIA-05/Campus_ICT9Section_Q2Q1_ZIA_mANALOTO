Crypto Currencies ZIA researched.

LITECOIN
Litecoin is digital money used online. It works like Bitcoin but is faster and cheaper. People use it to send payments worldwide. It was made in 2011 by Charlie Lee. Litecoin is secure and works on blockchain technology.
RIPPLE
Ripple is a digital payment system and cryptocurrency. Its coin is called XRP. Ripple helps banks and people send money fast worldwide. It is known for low fees and quick transactions. Ripple started in 2012 to improve global payments.
Etherum
Ethereum is a type of digital money and technology. It lets people create smart contracts and apps that run on blockchain. Its coin is called Ether (ETH). Ethereum is used for payments and building projects. It started in 2015 to improve blockchain features.
